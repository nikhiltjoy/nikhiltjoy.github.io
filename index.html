About

I am an Electrical Engineering graduate from the University of Waterloo, and I love data. I am currently based in Canada as a Data Scientist at Capital One, however have been lucky enough to call four countries my home.

I am an avid traveller, an amateur photographer, and a lover of food. My passion for food and photography shines through in my endless stream of food photos.

I am fascinated by languages and am always trying to expand my linguistic repertoire. I speak Malayalam and English fluently as my first languages and am continually improving my command of French and Swedish via Duolingo.
